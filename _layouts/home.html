I am a PhD researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics in the Department of Databases and Information Systems under the supervision of Simon Razniewski and Gerhard Weikum. I am also affiliated with the Saarbrücken Graduate School of Computer Science. My primary research areas are Web Science, Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval, particularly I'm interested in i) how count information is stored in text and knowledge bases and, ii) it's completeness. Read more about my research here and the department's here.
