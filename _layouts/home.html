Welcome

I am a PhD researcher at Max Plank Institute for Informatics in the Department of Databases and Information Systems under the supervision of Simon Razniewski. Knowledge base semantics and natural language processing are my research interests. As a PhD researcher I am also affiliated with the Saarbrücken Graduate School of Computer Science.
